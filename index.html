Overview

Identity Threat Hunter (ITH) is an end-to-end, cloud-native platform for detecting, analyzing, and explaining identity-driven threats. It simulates diverse authentication scenarios—such as credential stuffing, MFA fatigue, and honey-identity triggers—and ingests them into Elastic Security through a serverless pipeline on Google Cloud Run. Alerts are enriched in real time by Vertex AI, which generates human-readable ai.summary, and risk ai.confidence metrics for every detection.

Designed for enterprise scale, ITH connects the strengths of AI analytics with Elastic's detection engine to provide transparent, explainable identity intelligence. It supports rapid triage, security automation, and SOC enablement—all with synthetic, privacy-safe data for demonstration and benchmarking.

  • AI-generated summaries (ai.summary) and confidence (ai.confidence) per alert
  • Full Elastic integration with rules, risk scores, and timeline view
  • Cloud-run microservices: Event Generator, Ingestor, Analyst UI, Quantum Guardian
  • Safe synthetic data for demos—no production or PII exposure
  • Judge-ready interactive dashboards and one-click burst simulations

AI Module (Vertex AI Enrichment)

Each incoming event is enriched by Vertex AI (Gemini) before indexing to Elastic. The model adds:

  • ai.summary – natural‑language explanation of the risk
  • ai.confidence – 0‑1 score for the explanation
  • ai.details.scenario – appears on Elastic alerts for judge clarity

Google Cloud AI tools used.

How to Verify (in Kibana)

  1. Open Security → Alerts and click any ITH alert.
  2. Check ai.summary for AI‑generated context.
  3. Open Discover on ith-events* and filter ai.enriched:true.
  4. Confirm presence of ai.summary and ai.confidence.

Enterprise Solution

Identity Threat Hunter represents a paradigm shift in enterprise identity security. While traditional SIEM and identity solutions focus on rule-based detection or isolated anomaly scoring, ITH uniquely combines AI-driven contextual analysis with real-time behavioral correlation across the entire authentication lifecycle.

Market Gap & Innovation

Current enterprise solutions lack:

  • AI-native enrichment: Most tools add AI as an afterthought. ITH embeds Vertex AI at the ingestion layer, providing explainable intelligence (ai.summary, ai.confidence) for every event before indexing.
  • Synthetic threat simulation: Enterprises struggle to test detection efficacy without risking production systems. ITH's built-in event generator safely simulates credential stuffing, MFA bypass, impossible travel, and honey-identity scenarios.
  • Adaptive response automation: ITH connects detection to action—triggering OSquery investigations, host isolation, and adaptive workflows automatically when high-risk patterns emerge.
  • Cloud-native scalability: Unlike legacy on-prem tools, ITH runs entirely on serverless Cloud Run microservices, scaling elastically with zero infrastructure overhead.

Enterprise Deployment Model

ITH is designed for immediate enterprise adoption:

  • Zero-trust identity layer: Deploy as a centralized identity analytics hub ingesting events from Okta, Azure AD, AWS IAM, or any IdP via standard protocols (SAML, OIDC, Syslog).
  • SOC acceleration: Pre-built Elastic Security rules and AI explanations reduce mean-time-to-understand (MTTU) from hours to seconds. Analysts see contextualized alerts with automated triage recommendations.
  • Compliance & audit: AI-generated summaries provide auditable, human-readable explanations for every security decision—critical for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and regulatory frameworks.
  • Multi-tenant architecture: ITH's microservices model supports isolated tenants, custom rule sets per organization, and federated Elastic deployments for MSPs and large enterprises.

Measurable Impact

Detection Coverage:

  • 10+ critical identity attack vectors covered out-of-the-box
  • Impossible travel, credential stuffing, privilege escalation, MFA bypass

Key Differentiator: ITH is not just a detection tool—it's an identity security orchestration platform that unifies threat simulation, AI-powered detection, automated investigation, and response in a single cloud-native stack. No comparable integrated solution exists in the enterprise market today.
